Our wedding and reception will take place at Trump Winery, ten miles outside of downtown Charlottesille. We have arranged a discounted rate at the following hotels, and we will be providing bus transportation for all guests to and from both of these hotels to the wedding and reception venue.

The Omni Hotel

212 Ridge McIntire Road, $299+

Situated on Charlottesville's Downtown Mall, the Omni will put you in the heart of the town. Walk easily to over a hundred local restaurants and shops along one of Virginia's most beautiful pedestrian malls.
