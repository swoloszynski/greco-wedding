Our wedding and reception will take place at Trump Winery, ten miles outside of downtown Charlottesville. We have arranged a discounted rate at the following hotels, and we will be providing bus transportation for all guests from these hotels to the wedding and reception venue. If you do not plan to stay at one of the following hotels, please plan to arrive at either hotel with enough time to board the provided buses. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to the bride and groom.

The Omni Hotel

212 Ridge McIntire Road, $299+

Situated on Charlottesville's Downtown Mall, the Omni will put you in the heart of the town. Walk easily to over a hundred local restaurants and shops along one of Virginia's most beautiful pedestrian malls.

Phone: 434-971-5500

The Hilton Garden Inn

1792 Richmond Road, $219+

Located on a historic property in Pantops, the Hilton Garden Inn is a short drive from downtown. Here, you're closer to Thomas Jefferson's Monticello, Carter Mountain Orchard, and some of Nelson Country's 140+ breweries and vineyards.
